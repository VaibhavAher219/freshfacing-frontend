Our Story

Five decades of
Kalamazoo craft

1971

Golden Needle opens on East Michigan Avenue in the heart of downtown Kalamazoo — the same block it calls home today.

Since

Generations of Kalamazoo families have trusted us with their most cherished garments — wedding dresses, prom nights, first suits, heirlooms.

Today

Owner Fadi Fahs and the Golden Needle team bring the same personal attention and master-tailor standards to every piece that walks through the door.
