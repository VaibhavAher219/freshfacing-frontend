Our Story

A century of Reese craftsmanship

Five generations. One family. The longest-running HVAC legacy in Nashville. This is not a franchise — it's a calling passed from parent to child for over 100 years.

1899
C.H. Reese begins as a Tinsmith
The family trade starts with sheet metal work — roofing, guttering, and the beginnings of forced-air heating in Nashville homes.
1916
C.H. Reese opens his own business
Providing roofing, guttering, and hot air heating services to Nashville — the foundation of what would become the region's oldest HVAC family business.
1979
Bobby Reese, Inc. formally incorporated
The business expands into modern heating and air conditioning, incorporated under Bobby Reese with a full service and installation team.
1995
BBB Accreditation earned
Recognized by the Better Business Bureau for ethical business practices — an A+ rating that has been maintained ever since.
Today
Joseph S. Reese leads the 5th generation
The Reese family continues serving Nashville and all of Middle Tennessee — same values, same commitment, modern equipment and techniques.
